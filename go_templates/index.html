For the month of Movember, send us an email to have your reps tallied

Enter your email address to see how many reps you've put in!

Send your email to pullups-pushups-squats-situps@countmyreps.partkyle.io. In the subject, put your rep count, like so:
6, 24, 18, 12
This adds 6 pullups, 24 pushups, 18 airsquats, and 12 situps to your rep count.
